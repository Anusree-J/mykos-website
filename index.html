Why Mykos

Named after nature's
invisible infrastructure.

Underground fungal networks — mycelium — connect trees, share resources, and keep entire forests alive. No one sees them work. That's the point.

A chief of staff does the same thing for people. They connect the dots, move things forward, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks.

Here's what changed.

AI didn't reduce your workload — it expanded it. People work faster, take on broader tasks, and stretch into more hours. Engineers are doing design. PMs are writing code. Everyone's becoming a specialized generalist — more capable than ever, but tracking 500 threads.

That's a problem only CEOs used to have. And CEOs have chiefs of staff. Executive assistants. Support infrastructure.

You don't. Until now.
